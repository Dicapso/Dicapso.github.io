The Secret Life of Animals

Type: AI Short-form Video

This project explores a viral AI short-form video concept where animals secretly live human-like lives. The content is optimized for YouTube Shorts, Reels, and TikTok with a strong hook in the first second.

What I did

  • Created the creative concept
  • Wrote structured AI prompts
  • Focused on pacing and short-form storytelling

Example Prompt

Create an 8–10 second cinematic vertical AI video showing animals acting like humans in secret moments. High contrast lighting, fast cuts, emotional mood. Aspect ratio 9:16.
